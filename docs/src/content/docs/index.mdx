## Why srs?

**srs** is a spaced-repetition terminal application. Your cards are ordinary
Markdown files you own and version-control, and reviews are scheduled by
[FSRS](https://github.com/open-spaced-repetition) — a modern, evidence-based
algorithm that adapts to how well you actually remember each card.
